March 16, 1858 - Feburay 26, 1913

Bud Fowler

He was the first known African American baseball player and broke the color line for organized baseball. In 1895, he, along with, teammate Grant "Home Run" Johson founded the Page Fence Giants, a dominant pre-Negro League team.
